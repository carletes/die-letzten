Proofers, please pay attention to the following things:

  • Please note that using WordCheck is mandatory in P2 and P3 and strongly recommended in P1!
  • If there are spaces around em-dashes (--) in the original, please retain or add them.
  • Proof ellipses (...) using the number of dots you see in the scan. Always put a space before the ellipsis, except if it follows another punctuation/quotation mark. Always put a space after the ellipsis, except if it precedes another punctuation/quotation mark. Never put spaces in between the individual dots.
  • Please change German double quotes to »Guillemets«.
  • Please use greater than and less than signs for single quotes, like >this<.
  • Please restore ck in words hyphenated as k-k.
  • Don't introduce any formatting in the P rounds!

Formatters, I am not aware of any deviations from the standard guidelines.
